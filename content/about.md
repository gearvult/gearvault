## About GearVault

GearVault provides in-depth reviews, head-to-head comparisons, and buying guides for musical instruments and pro audio equipment.

We focus on helping musicians and producers make informed purchasing decisions, especially for high-ticket gear where the right choice matters most.

### What We Cover

- **Electric & Acoustic Guitars** - From $500 to $5,000+
- **Studio Equipment** - Audio interfaces, monitors, microphones, preamps
- **Digital Pianos & Keyboards** - Stage pianos, workstations, synthesizers
- **Live Sound** - PA systems, powered speakers, mixing consoles
- **Drums** - Acoustic kits, electronic drums, cymbals
- **Bass & Amps** - Bass guitars, guitar amps, bass amps
